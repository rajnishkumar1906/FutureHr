# FutureHR - Employee Tasks

## What Employees Actually Do in FutureHR

### 1. Dashboard
- See a personalized welcome (e.g., "Welcome back, John!")
- View stats relevant to them
- See charts for overview

### 2. Attendance
- View their own attendance records
- See their own check-in/check-out times
- See their own attendance status (Present/Absent)

### 3. Payroll
- View their own payroll slips/records
- See their own salary details: basic salary, allowances, deductions, and net salary
- Check if their payroll is paid or pending

### 4. Performance Goals
- View their own performance goals and KPIs
- See their progress on goals
- See their target dates for goals

### 5. Account
- Can log in and log out
- Can switch between dark mode and light mode

---
Key Note for Employees:
- Employees CANNOT see other employees' data
- Employees CANNOT access any recruitment features (candidates, resume screening, etc.)
- Employees can only see and manage their OWN personal HR info
