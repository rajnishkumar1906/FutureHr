# FutureHR - HR Recruiter Tasks

## Full List of HR Recruiter Responsibilities in FutureHR

### 1. Dashboard & Overview
- Access the HR Recruiter dashboard
- View real-time charts for candidates and recruitment stats
- See counts for total candidates, attendance records, employees, and payroll records

### 2. Candidate Management
- View all job candidates in the system
- Add new candidates to the recruitment pipeline
- See candidate details (name, email, phone, skills, status, etc.)

### 3. AI-Powered Recruitment
- Use AI to screen resumes against job descriptions
- Get detailed AI resume screening results (score, skills match, experience match, analysis, recommendation)
- Use AI to do full candidate evaluations (strengths, weaknesses, skill gaps, summary, recommendation)
- Do AI-based voice screening of candidates (transcription, communication score, confidence score, analysis)

### 4. System Access
- Access all recruitment-focused pages: Dashboard, Candidates, Resume Screening, AI Evaluation, Voice Screening
- Can switch between dark and light mode using the toggle

---
Note: HR Recruiter does NOT have access to Employees, Attendance, Payroll, or Performance pages (those are Admin/Employee only)
